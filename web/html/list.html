Steps to Make a Cup of Tea (Ordered List)

  1. Boil water
  2. Add tea leaves to the pot
    • 1 teaspoon per cup
    • Additional spoon for the pot
  3. Pour boiling water into the pot
  4. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes
  5. Pour into a cup and add milk or sugar if desired
